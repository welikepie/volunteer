Events looking for Volunteers this month
  Sketching is a core skill and tool of any designer. It is a quick and cheap way to ideate, develop and iterate on possible design solutions, both alone and with a group. On the 11th of February 2014, Eva-Lotta Lamm will be hosting a night class on sketching interfaces. In this workshop you will take a closer look at when and how to use sketching in the design process and how to make your sketches communicate more clearly and efficiently. This workshop is ideal for Designers, Developers, Product Managers or anyone who is involved in creating websites or applications and wants to add sketching to their range of tools. All skill levels are welcome and no previous sketching skills or knowledge are necessary.
